Directions:

Upon starting the game, you will choose your starting supplies with your initial budget of $20. Your supplies will include cups, lemons, sugar, and ice.

When a new day starts, you will be shown the conditions of that day. These include the temperature, climate, and total potential customers for the day based on the two.

The temperature can range from 55-92 degrees Fahrenheit, and the climate can be Sunny, Sunny and Dry, Cloudy, or Rainy.

Based on the conditions, it is your job to choose how to manage your supplies and decide what goes into your cups of lemonade at the beginning of each day.

Earn as much profit as you can in 7 days and beat your high score!
